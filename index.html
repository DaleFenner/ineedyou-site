MISSION

Bring light to mental health.

This brand exists for the people who smile in public, stay quiet about what hurts, and carry more than anyone realizes. INeedYOU is about connection, awareness, honesty, and building the biggest mental health awareness community possible.

you don't have to pretend here.

IMPORTANT

Support and safety.

INeedYOU is peer support and community — not therapy, counseling, or emergency crisis care. If someone is in immediate danger or thinking about self-harm, they should contact trained crisis support right away.

Need immediate help?

Call or text 988 in the U.S. for the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline.

For immediate danger, call emergency services right away.
